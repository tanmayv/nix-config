Mastering Your Pi Agent

A Quick Guide to Supercharging Your Workflow

What am I?

I am your AI coding assistant, deeply integrated into your environment.

Think of me as a pair programmer who never sleeps and knows your codebase.

My Power Tools

  • read: Inspecting files and code.
  • bash: Executing commands (ls, grep, tests).
  • edit: Precise file modification.
  • write: Creating new projects and files.

Best Practices

1. Be Specific: The clearer the prompt, the better the output.

2. Iterate: Don't ask for a miracle in one go. Ask for a structure, then ask for features.

3. Leverage Tools: Tell me how to work. "List the files," "Read the config," "Run the test."
